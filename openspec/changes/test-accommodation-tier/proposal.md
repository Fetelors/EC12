# Test Feature: Accommodation Tier Selection

## What are we building?
A simple feature that allows artists to select their accommodation tier (budget, standard, premium) during the intake process.

## Why?
To collect accommodation preferences early so the advancing team can book hotels efficiently.

## Scope
- Add accommodation tier field to the artist intake form
- Store tier selection in the artist record
- Display selected tier in artist summary view
- No special logic or automation (Phase 1)

## Success Criteria
- Field appears on intake form
- Tier is saved and retrievable
- No blocking issues with existing code
